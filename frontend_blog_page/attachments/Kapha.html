KAPHA

Represents the earth and water elements, governing structure and lubrication. Kapha Prakriti, one of the three primary constitutions in Ayurveda, is characterized by the dominance of the earth and water elements. Individuals with Kapha Prakriti are often described as calm, grounded, and nurturing, with a stable and consistent temperament. They tend to have a sturdy build, with strong muscles and thick, oily skin. Kapha types are known for their endurance, patience, and loyalty. When out of balance, they may experience weight gain, sluggishness, and respiratory issues. It's important for Kapha individuals to engage in regular physical activity, maintain a light and warm diet, and incorporate stimulating practices to balance Kapha dosha and promote overall vitality and well-being.
