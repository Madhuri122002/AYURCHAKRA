PITTA

Represents the fire and water elements, governing digestion and metabolism. Pitta Prakriti, another fundamental constitution in Ayurveda, is characterized by the dominance of the fire and water elements. Individuals with Pitta Prakriti are often ambitious, focused, and intelligent, with a strong drive and leadership qualities. They tend to have a medium build, with warm body temperature and a sharp intellect. Pitta types are prone to conditions related to excess heat, such as inflammation, acidity, and skin rashes when out of balance. To maintain equilibrium, it's important for Pitta individuals to stay cool, both physically and emotionally. Cooling practices, a balanced diet, and stress management techniques can help pacify Pitta dosha and promote overall health and well-being.
