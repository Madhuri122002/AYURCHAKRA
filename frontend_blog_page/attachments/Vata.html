VATA

Represents the air and space elements, governing movement and communication. Vata Prakriti, one of the three primary constitutions in Ayurveda, is characterized by the dominance of the air and space elements. Individuals with Vata Prakriti tend to be creative, energetic, and enthusiastic, often displaying quick thinking and spontaneity. Physically, they tend to have a light build, with dry skin and hair. When out of balance, Vata types may experience anxiety, insomnia, and digestive issues. It's essential for them to follow a routine, maintain warmth, and indulge in grounding activities to stay balanced and healthy. Ayurvedic practices such as warm oil massages and a nourishing diet can help pacify Vata dosha and promote overall well-being.
